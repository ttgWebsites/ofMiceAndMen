Basic Info

  • Title
  • Of Mice and Men
  • Author
  • John Steinbeck
  • Published
  • The novel was first published in 1937 by Covici, Friede, Inc.
  • Genre
  • Fiction, Tragedy.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in the 1930's in an area of California, south of the city of Soledad.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is narrated in the third-person omniscient point of view from an unspecified narrator. The narrator takes the point of view of different characters throughout.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict in the novel revolves around Lennie. Lennie is slightly mentally challenged, doesn't no his own strength, and cannot control himself. George feels responsible for Lennie as he has been caring for him for a long time and must clean up after his mistakes. In the end George must deal with a terrible accident caused by Lennie's carelessness and must confront the problem that Lennie being alive poses.
