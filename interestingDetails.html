Interesting Details

  • The author, John Steinbeck, won a Nobel Prize for literature in 1962, this is one of the highest honors one can receive for literature, if not the highest.
  • Steinbeck's books often focused on the poor and working class citizens of California. Of Mice and Men does not stray from this theme one bit. Steinbeck, being a Californian himself, viewed the plight of the average man and woman as very important, and worked to make there story known.
  • Steinbeck grew up working on various farms as a hired hand, he never was forced to live a life as a migrant worker, but his experiences brought him close to many and helped him gain perspective.
